Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup

Description

This is the ultimate comfort food. It features a dark, savory broth seasoned with soy sauce and five-spice, filled with melt-in-your-mouth beef and chewy noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef shank or chuck, cubed
  • 4 cups beef stock
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 star anise
  • Fresh wheat noodles

Steps

  1. Blanch beef in boiling water for 2 minutes, then rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large pot, sauté ginger, garlic, and onions until fragrant.
  3. Add the beef, stock, soy sauce, and star anise.
  4. Simmer on low for 2.5 hours until the beef is very tender.
  5. Cook noodles according to package instructions, place in a bowl, and ladle the soup over them.
