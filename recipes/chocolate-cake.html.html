One-Bowl Chocolate Cake

Description

This is a "no-fail" recipe for the moistest chocolate cake you've ever had. It's incredibly easy because you mix everything in one single bowl!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt directly in the bowl.
  3. Add oil, vanilla, and water; whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
