Creamy Pesto Shrimp

Description

This recipe features tender shrimp tossed in a rich, velvety cream sauce infused with basil pesto. It's a restaurant-quality meal that comes together in less than 20 minutes.

Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner when served over your favorite pasta or a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup pesto

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Add shrimp and cook until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and pesto.
  5. Simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
